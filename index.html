MiHub is proud to be a partner in the GCIP 2.0 Hubs and Spokes Initiative

Under the GCIP 2.0 Hubs and Spokes initiative, the Accelerator is being delivered in collaboration with nine cleantech implementing partners across South Africa. These partners include leading innovation hubs, universities, and business incubators that provide tailored acceleration support, expert mentorship, and access to valuable networks and funding opportunities.

MiHub as an implementing partner, will continue to contribute to the advancement of clean technology innovation and entrepreneurship in South Africa.
